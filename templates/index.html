About the Notebook Program

The Notebook Program is a volunteer-driven initiative focused on mentoring students from underserved communities, especially in North Chennai. We support children from Class 6 to 12, offering guidance in all academic subjects, English, career awareness, and exam preparation.

Through online and offline sessions, our mentors help students build confidence, improve learning outcomes, and dream bigger.

If you're passionate about teaching and creating impact, join us as a volunteer or intern!
